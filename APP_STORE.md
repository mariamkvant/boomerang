## App Name

## Subtitle

## Description
Boomerang is a community platform where people exchange skills and services without money. Share what you're good at, earn boomerangs (points), and use them to get help when you need it.

How it works:
1. Share your skills — list what you can do: gardening, guitar lessons, cooking, tech help, home repair, and more
2. Help others and earn boomerangs — when you complete a service, you earn boomerangs automatically
3. Get help back — use your boomerangs to request services from others in your community

Features:
- 20+ service categories with 100+ subcategories
- Smart points system with fair multipliers based on skill level
- Direct messaging with emoji support and video calling
- Communities — create or join local groups for your neighborhood, school, or workplace
- Help Wanted board — post what you need, let helpers come to you
- Trust scores and Superhelper badges
- Availability calendar and booking system
- Multi-language support (English, French, German, Portuguese, Spanish, Italian, Georgian, Luxembourgish)
- Referral system — invite friends, both earn bonus boomerangs
- No money involved — just people helping people
